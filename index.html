about us

Toledo Minds was conceived during the COVID quarantine of 2020, when a group of high school students watched their younger siblings struggle with online learning. They subsequently created a free STEM camp open to all students grades 3-6. The camp teaches, reinforces, and augments fundamental math skills as well as scientific concepts and methods. The camp is highly interactive, emphasizing the fun of learning. Now entering its fourth year, Toledo Minds sets the highest standard for STEM education.
