2024 summer program

STEM camp

Toledo Minds hosts a week-long summer camp designed to develop STEM skills. The program meets from 9-1 Monday through Friday, and students engage in a range of fun STEM activities organized by high school students. Students are grouped by ability level, and receive small-group and one-on-one attention.

325 N Michigan St, Toledo, OH 43604

August 5-9, 2024

Available to students in the Toledo area entering grades 3-6

This camp is completely free!
